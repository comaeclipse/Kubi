# SafeVision Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** February 18, 2026
**Last Updated:** February 18, 2026

SafeVision ("the App," "we," "us," or "our") is a parent-managed video curation application designed for children. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use SafeVision. We are committed to complying with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), and Apple's App Store Guidelines for Kids Category apps.

---

## 1. Overview

SafeVision allows parents or guardians ("Administrators") to curate YouTube video content for children ("Child Users") by selecting approved channels and managing viewer profiles. The App is designed with children's privacy as a core principle.

**Key commitments:**
- We do **not** collect personal information from children
- We do **not** display advertisements
- We do **not** use third-party analytics, tracking, or behavioral profiling
- We do **not** share any user data with third parties
- All content is parent-curated and parent-controlled

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information Provided by Administrators

| Data | Purpose | Storage |
|------|---------|---------|
| **Admin PIN** | Restrict access to administrative functions (adding channels, managing profiles, hiding videos) | Stored as a one-way bcrypt hash in our database. The plaintext PIN is never stored or logged. |
| **Profile names** | Distinguish between child viewer profiles (e.g., "Emma," "Jake") | Stored in our database. We recommend using first names only or nicknames. |
| **Profile avatar colors** | Visual identification of profiles | Stored as a color code in our database. |

### 2.2 Information Generated by App Usage

| Data | Purpose | Storage |
|------|---------|---------|
| **Video watch progress** | Resume playback where the child left off | Stored per profile as a number of seconds watched, linked to a YouTube video ID. |
| **Active profile selection** | Remember which profile is in use on the device | Stored locally on the device via browser localStorage. Not transmitted to any server. |

### 2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We explicitly do **not** collect:
- Names, email addresses, phone numbers, or physical addresses of children or adults
- Photos, videos, or audio recordings of users
- Precise or coarse geolocation data
- Device identifiers, advertising IDs, or hardware serial numbers
- Browsing history outside of the App
- Contact lists or social media accounts
- Biometric data
- Financial or payment information
- Any data for the purpose of behavioral advertising or user profiling

---

## 3. How We Use Information

All collected information is used solely to operate the App:

- **Admin PIN hash** — To authenticate the parent/guardian for administrative actions
- **Profile names and colors** — To display the profile selection screen and personalize the viewing experience
- **Watch progress** — To allow children to resume videos from where they stopped

We do **not** use any information for:
- Advertising or marketing
- Behavioral analysis or profiling
- Sale or transfer to third parties
- Training machine learning models
- Any purpose unrelated to the core App functionality

- The video stream is delivered directly from YouTube's servers to the user's device

## 5. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

SafeVision is designed for use by children under the direction and supervision of a parent or guardian.

### 5.1 Verifiable Parental Consent

- All administrative functions (adding channels, creating profiles, hiding videos, changing settings) are protected behind a parent-set PIN
- The PIN acts as the parental consent mechanism — only a parent or guardian who set the PIN can manage the App
- Child Users interact only with pre-approved, parent-curated content
- Child Users cannot create accounts, enter personal information, or communicate with others

### 5.2 Data Minimization

We collect only the minimum data necessary to operate the App:
- Profile names (which may be nicknames or pseudonyms)
- Profile colors (for visual identification)
- Watch progress (seconds watched per video)

No data that could reasonably identify a specific child is collected.

### 5.3 No Behavioral Tracking of Children

- We do not build behavioral profiles of Child Users
- We do not track children across apps or websites
- We do not use cookies or similar technologies for tracking purposes
- Video recommendations are based solely on video title keyword matching, not on individual viewing history

### 5.4 Parental Rights Under COPPA

Parents and guardians have the right to:
- **Review** all data associated with their child's profile (accessible through the Admin dashboard)
- **Delete** their child's profile and all associated watch progress at any time via the Admin dashboard
- **Refuse further collection** by deleting the profile or uninstalling the App
- **Contact us** with questions or requests regarding their child's data (see Section 10)

### 5.5 Data Retention for Children

- Watch progress data is retained only as long as the profile exists
- Deleting a profile permanently and immediately deletes all associated watch progress (enforced via database cascade deletion)
- There is no archival or backup retention of deleted profile data

---

## 6. Data Security

We implement the following security measures:

- **PIN hashing** — Admin PINs are hashed using bcrypt (10 rounds) before storage. Plaintext PINs are never stored or logged.
- **HTTP-only cookies** — Admin session cookies are HTTP-only (inaccessible to JavaScript), Secure (HTTPS-only in production), and SameSite=Strict (CSRF protection).
- **Parameterized queries** — All database queries use parameterized statements via Drizzle ORM, preventing SQL injection.
- **HTTPS encryption** — All data in transit is encrypted via TLS/HTTPS.
- **Minimal attack surface** — No user accounts, no passwords, no email storage, no payment processing.
- **Cascade deletion** — Deleting a profile or channel automatically deletes all associated child data.

---

## 7. Data Retention and Deletion

| Data | Retention | Deletion |
|------|-----------|----------|
| Admin PIN hash | Until changed or App data is cleared | Overwritten when PIN is changed |
| Profiles | Until deleted by Administrator | Immediate, permanent, cascading |
| Watch progress | Until profile is deleted | Automatic on profile deletion |
| Channel/video metadata | Until channel is removed by Administrator | Immediate, permanent, cascading |
| localStorage (active profile) | Until browser data is cleared | Cleared on profile switch or browser clear |

We do not maintain backups of deleted data. Deletion is permanent.
